Harsha Kokel is a Research Scientist at IBM Research. Her research centers on automating sequential decision-making through the application of Artificial Intelligence to enhance semantic automation within structured domains. Accordingly, her work involves evaluating and enhancing AI models' capabilities for search, planning, reasoning, and generalization. To learn more about her research, visit the publications page.
