I am a Ph.D. candidate working with Prof. Sriraam Natarajan and the StARLinG Lab at The University of Texas at Dallas. Before pursuing graduate studies, I provided content management solutions for four years as a software engineer; one year at Amadeus, Bangalore, India, and three years at Publicis Sapient, Bangalore, India. I earned my bachelor's degree in Information and Communication Technology (ICT) from DAIICT, Gandhinagar, India.

My research focuses on leveraging domain-specific expert knowledge to improve generalization in various AI/ML approaches. I am interested in knowledge-guided learning for structured, relational domains. I am also interested in sequential decision-making problems and, specifically, exploring the combination of planning and reinforcement learning for structured domains.
